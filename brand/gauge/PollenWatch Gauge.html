Status-hub style (the bloom's center carries the active color), mixed shown with rim marks at every level, pollen grains that grow with severity, and gray needle-less missing states. Pollen grains scale with level

More pollen in the air = worse. None sheds nothing; low a few; high a full ring — reinforcing severity beyond color alone.

Mixed — sources disagree

No single level to point at: needle removed, slate marks sit at all three levels ("the reading spans these"), hollow hub. The caption can optionally name the span.

No-data & unknown — never "low"

An empty reading is not a safe-low reading. Missing states go neutral gray, no needle, no pollen — shown beside a real Low so the difference is obvious.

Low / in season — green, needle + a few grains. A real, safe reading.
Unknown — dashed gray track, hollow hub. Entity exists, not enough history yet.
No data — faint gray + diagonal slash. Source absent / disconnected.
